# ISO 27001 Implementation Lab

## Overview

This repository contains a practical ISO/IEC 27001 implementation project created to develop hands-on Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) experience.

The project simulates the implementation of an Information Security Management System (ISMS) for a fictional organisation, following the principles and requirements of ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

The objective was to understand how organisations establish information security governance, identify and manage risks, select security controls, conduct internal audits and perform management reviews to support continual improvement.

---

## Project Objectives

* Understand the structure and requirements of ISO/IEC 27001
* Apply the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability (CIA) principles
* Develop practical risk management documentation
* Gain experience with Annex A controls and the Statement of Applicability
* Understand the relationship between risk assessment, risk treatment and security controls
* Simulate internal audit and management review activities

## Documents Included

### 1. ISMS Scope

Defines the boundaries and applicability of the Information Security Management System.

### 2. Information Security Policy

High-level information security policy outlining objectives, responsibilities and security commitments.

### 3. Asset Register

Inventory of organisational assets including ownership, classification and criticality.

### 4. Risk Register

Identification and assessment of information security risks affecting organisational assets.

### 5. Risk Treatment Plan

Documented actions designed to reduce identified risks to acceptable levels.

### 6. Statement of Applicability (SoA)

Selection and justification of applicable ISO 27001 Annex A controls.

### 7. Internal Audit Report

Assessment of ISMS effectiveness and compliance with documented requirements.

### 8. Management Review Minutes

Review of ISMS performance, audit findings, risks and improvement opportunities.

## Key Lessons Learned

Through this project I developed a practical understanding of how ISO 27001 documentation connects together:

Asset Register → Risk Register → Risk Treatment Plan → Annex A Controls → Internal Audit → Management Review

The project demonstrated that information security is not solely a technical discipline. Effective security relies on governance, risk management, documentation, accountability and continual improvement.

My previous experience as a Compliance Team Leader provided a strong foundation in audits, corrective actions, investigations, process reviews and compliance activities, which helped me understand how these principles are applied within information security programmes.
